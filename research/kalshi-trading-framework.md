# Kalshi Trading Framework

Imported from previous instance. See /tmp/old-md/ for full version.

## Core Strategy
1. **Calibration arbitrage** — 85-95% favorites often underpriced
2. **Cross-market comparison** — Kalshi vs Polymarket for price discrepancies
3. **Information edge** — Government data feeds, real-time APIs beat news
4. **Market microstructure** — New listings and thin markets = opportunity

## Pre-Trade Checklist
- Edge > 2x fees
- Position < 10% bankroll
- Net P&L positive after fees
- Resolution date/criteria confirmed

## Position Sizing (Kelly Criterion)
- Use fractional Kelly (25-50%)
- Max single bet: 10% bankroll
- With $20 bankroll: $2 max per trade

## Success Metrics
- Win rate > 60%
- Positive Brier score vs market
- No drawdowns > 50%
- 20% monthly return target ($4/month proof of concept)
